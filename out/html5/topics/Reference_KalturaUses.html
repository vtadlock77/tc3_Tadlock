The Do's and Do Not's of Using Kaltura

Information detailing when it is and is not appropriate to use Kaltura.

  • DO access Kaltura through Canvas and Kaltura MediaSpace.

  • DO use Kaltura for files that will be actively used on an ongoing basis.

  • DO NOT use Kaltura for videos not related to teaching, learning, research, or outreach. For those videos, use Youtube.

  • DO NOT use Kaltura for storing Protected Health Information (PHI). Use Box Secure Storage instead.

  • DO NOT use Kaltura as a platform for archiving media files.
