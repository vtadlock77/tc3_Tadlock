Highlights

This section lists the highlights of Kaltura.

  • Users can upload, record, and manage video and audio files.
  • Use the Kaltura Capture desktop recorder to record your screen and/or desktop.
  • Videos published in Canvas courses are only viewable by other people enrolled in those courses.
  • Uploaded video/audio files receive auto-generated draft captions through automatic speech recognition.
  • Users can search captions within and across videos as a way to review content and learn more.
  • Viewer analytics let you know who is watching videos and for how long.
  • Unlimited user storage and bandwidth.
